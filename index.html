/ 01 — ETHOS

Crypto was meant to be a casino tool.

Somewhere along the way, wallets became casinos. They sprouted swap buttons, credit-card on-ramps, NFT galleries, yield farms, and "trending tokens" carousels designed to make you trade until you bleed.

Each of those features is also a door. A door for a phishing site. A door for a malicious contract. A door for a hacker who only needs you to click once. We slammed every one of those doors shut.

Spectra holds your coins. It sends your coins. It stakes your coins. It recovers your coins when something goes wrong. That is the entire job.

What Spectra is

  • A wallet to hold
  • A tool for payment
  • A staking client
  • A recovery toolkit
  • Open source. Auditable. Reproducible.

What Spectra is not

  • An exchange
  • A DEX aggregator
  • A "yield optimizer"
  • A meme-token launcher
  • A get-rich-quick machine

/ 03 — DIAGNOSTICS

The first wallet that tells you what's wrong before you have to ask.

Most wallets are black boxes. When something breaks — a stuck transaction, a desync, a bad fee estimate — you're left guessing. Spectra ships with a live diagnostics panel and a structured log viewer that turns "it's broken" into "here's the line and here's the fix."

  • Health checks: peer count, sync depth, mempool latency, key integrity, RPC pings.
  • Auto-repair: common failures (stale UTXO cache, orphaned tx) repaired in one click.
  • Exportable bug reports: redacted, signed, ready to file as a GitHub issue.

/ 04 — LEGACY & RECOVERY

    
Got an old wallet from 2013?
Bring it home.

    

      Spectra is built with a fanatical commitment to backwards compatibility. If
      your coins exist on-chain, we will find a way to read them. We support legacy
      formats most modern wallets quietly dropped years ago.
    


    

      

        
·01

        
Wallet imports

        
wallet.dat, Electrum 1.x & 2.x, Multibit, Armory, Bither, Mycelium, Blockchain.info v2/v3, Coinomi, MEW keystore, JSON keystore (v3), raw WIF, BIP-38 encrypted keys.

      

      

        
·02

        
Lost-fund scanner

        
Give us a seed phrase and we'll grind every common derivation path — BIP-32, 44, 49, 84, 86, m/0', m/0/0, Ledger Live, Electrum, custom — and surface every UTXO we find.

      

      

        
·03

        
Forgotten passwords

        
Local, offline brute-forcer with custom rules: typo correction, common substitutions, dictionary + mask. Your machine. Your patience. No cloud.
